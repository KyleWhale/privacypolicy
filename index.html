Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2024
Last Updated: January 1, 2024

Welcome to Focutodo ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google or Facebook, we collect your name, email address, and profile information
  • Usage Data: Focus session duration, app usage patterns, and feature interactions
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Analytics Data: App performance, crash reports, and usage statistics via Firebase Analytics
  • Advertising Data: Ad interaction data and preferences for personalized advertising
  • Technical Data: IP address, app version, device settings

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain the focus timer functionality
  • Sync your focus data across devices
  • Send push notifications for focus reminders (with your consent)
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
  • Provide customer support

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Analytics, crash reporting, remote configuration, and messaging
  • IronSource: Ad mediation and monetization
  • Kochava: Attribution and analytics
  • Facebook SDK: Social login and analytics
  • Google Sign-In: Authentication services
  • Ad Networks: InMobi, Vungle, Pangle, Smaato, Tencent, Unity Ads for advertising

3.2 Data Sharing Practices

  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties
  • Anonymous usage data may be shared with analytics providers
  • Advertising partners may receive anonymized data for ad targeting
  • We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
  • Secure authentication mechanisms

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Account Management

  • Access: Request access to your personal information
  • Correction: Update or correct your account information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data

5.2 Privacy Controls

  • Notifications: Disable push notifications in app settings
  • Analytics: Opt-out of analytics tracking (may limit functionality)
  • Advertising: Limit ad tracking through device settings

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

7. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

8. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Usage Data: Retained for up to 2 years for analytics purposes
  • Crash Reports: Retained for up to 1 year for debugging
  • Marketing Data: Retained until you opt-out or request deletion

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy in the app
  • Sending a push notification (if enabled)
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

10. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Union, our legal basis for processing includes:

  • Consent: For marketing communications and non-essential features
  • Contract: To provide the focus timer service
  • Legitimate Interest: For analytics and app improvement
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with applicable laws

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@focutodo.com
  • App: Use the "Contact Support" feature in app settings
  • Response Time: We aim to respond within 48 hours
